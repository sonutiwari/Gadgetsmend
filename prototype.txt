Overview: 

This is a service based company which will provide repairing service for

each and every electronic gadget to the customers with pick and drop service.This will act

as a platform with "Service center of all brands under one shelter".Any type of problem

either hardware or software will be under its grip.The only brand with service center of all

brands under one shelter will provide best service with warranty to repairing works of

minimum one month.To the most possibility for any problem we will try to solve it onsite

first.Highly skilled Engineers and best policies for customers will be the silver part of this

project.



Opportunity:
Whenever any gadgets gets ruined,not to worry,we will make it as it

was.Being a "Doctor of gadgets" we will provide service to the users in taking care of

their gadgets (with warranty) in all possible way we can and being the 1st one with these

services in market we can have a best response from our customers which will define our

opportunity.
